About TF2D

TF2D is a fan-made 2D adaptation of the legendary Team Fortress 2, built from scratch in Godot Engine. Experience fast-paced team-based combat with unique classes, diverse weapons, and strategic gameplay - all in a top-down perspective!

Fight as Scout, Soldier, Pyro, Heavy, or Spy in intense 6v6 battles. Master each class's unique abilities, from the Spy's invisibility cloak to the Pyro's devastating flamethrower!
